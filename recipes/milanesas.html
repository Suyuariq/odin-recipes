Milanesas


The argentine milanesa is seasoned with garlic and a little parsley, whereas the cotoletta is a bit simpler It is typically served hot and served with french fries or mashed potatoes.

There are several versions of milanesa including chicken milanesa, fish, soy or eggplant. .

Ingredients

  • Veal steaks, Milanese cut or thinly sliced
  • Eggs
  • Bread crumbs
  • Vegetable or sunflower oil
  • One bunch of parsley
  • Garlic cloves
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Step by step instructions

  1. Season Veal Steaks and Prepare Beaten Eggs Mixture
  2. Soak and Bread The Veal Cuts
  3. Fry Until Golden Brown
  4. Enjoy!
